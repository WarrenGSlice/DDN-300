The Cradle Of Existence



The Cradle of existence tells the story of a young man who falls asleep and is giving a magical power in his sleep. The power to use a pencil to draw imagination into reality. Upon using these rare gifts, the child attracts the attention of the most dangerous beings in the multiverse. This ignites a struggle between good and evil where the very nature of existence is at stake. Who wins in this epic battle of good vs. evil? Purchase today to find out how the story ends.

Jay currently resides in Longview Washington with his wife and children. He has been writing for several years now and is on the precipice of his first published book. In his spare time he likes to play video games, read, and go on picnics with his family.


Jay's biggest influences are Stephen King and Neil Gaimen. They are famous for their epic fantasy, horror and adventure stories. Their plots are known to be incredibly creative and leave the reader wanting for me. Jay's writing style is very similiar although more closely resembles Neil Gaimen. Jay of course adds to this with his own unique writing style and personality. Jay is famous for his sense of humor and creatively. He is also a fan of Lovecraft and his books often include creatures and entities that are like something out of a Lovecraft novel. He has even adopted the Lovecraft symbol and changed it slightly for the mock cover of his book "The Cradle of Existence".


Eventually Jay's website is going to be for everything he writes and will all exist under the umbrella of this website. Currently the book that Jay is writing now is about a boy who is visited in his dreams and given a magical pencil that can create what he draws in the material world and an angel who recruits some damaged fellow to murder this character. The Baddie fights the main character and angels show up to pursuade him against his actions. Eventually we learn what is truely at stake in this epic battle, the entire world! Find out how it ends and buy the book Today!


This is just a small example of the writing in one of Jay's Books. This will be his magnum opus, his dark tower. The character that is going to be in all of the books is called the old one and there is a lot of back story. Basically something will happen to connect all the universes and certain gods of other mythos make it through to the different realms and reak havic with the different characters in the different books. They will al have different plot lines, but have some parallels that connect them altogether.


We are all very excited about where this journey is headed and we would be pleased to have you join along for the ride. Jay is a good man, a good soul and we all look forward to watching him grow, learn and succeed along this journey. Thank you so much for visiting this page and helping to support us. Please feel free to reach out to Jay and let him know what you think. You, the fans, are everything and we love you! <3


Sincerely,

Jay Jellum
